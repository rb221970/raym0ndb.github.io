My Final Project

Here you will find the work my partner and I have created for our final project in CSC106.
The game is called "Escape Route", and is an impossible style type of game where a user tries to last for as long as they can.
To play, the user is to choose a character to play as (which are bitmojis of my partner and I), and use the arrow keys to navigate
around the obstacles for as long as they can. The game is intentionally difficult and is not intended to be 'won'. The score is
to be kept by the user which is the amount in time in seconds they have lasted while trying to dodge the obstacles.
